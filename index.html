About Me

💻 I am a fourth-year undergraduate at IIIS, Tsinghua University (a.k.a. Yao Class, directed by the Turing Award Laureate Andrew C. Yao). I major in Computer Science and Technology, and minor in Chinese Language and Literature.

📚 My research interests are broad. I have worked on topics related to foundation models and optimization theory. During my undergraduate, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Simon S. Du at UW and Prof. Huazhe Xu at Tsinghua.

👽 My mind is open. If you have any ideas which we both might be interested in, feel free to reach out.
