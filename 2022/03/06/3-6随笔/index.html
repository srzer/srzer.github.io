我们从坚果剥出时间并教它走路.

3.6随笔

想要求：
$$
\int_0^{+\infty}e^{-\frac{t^4}{4}}dt
$$
EI:

Gamma函数 换元$t^4=u$.

$e^{-t^2}$能算是因为pi这个常量是处理$I_2$的时候定义的，如果我们定义了一个$I_4$上类似的常量就能表示$t^4$对应积出来的结果，但也就是给$gamma(1/4)$起了个名字。

Let $d>1$. Consider the unit radius ball under $l_4$ norm in $R^d$ de
fined as follows:
$$
K={x=(x_1,x_2,…,x_d)\in R^d|x_1^4+…+x_d^4\le 1}.
$$

For any $c>0$, prove that the fraction of volume $K$ outside the slab $|x_1|\le \frac{c}{d^{\frac{1}{4}}}$ is at most $e^{\frac{-c^4}{8}}$.

这个题目有点困扰我，然而“智应数 p1 的坑点在于它这个放缩不是任何一个书上或课上讲过的思路”。

By symmetry we just need to prove that at most a $e^{\frac{-c^4}{8}}$ fraction of the half of the ball with $x_1\ge 0$ has $x_1\ge \frac{c}{d^{\frac{1}{4}}}$. Let A denote the portion of the ball with $x_1\ge \frac{c}{d^{\frac{1}{4}}}$ and let H denote the upper hemisphere.\
$$
V(A)\int_\frac{c}{d^\frac{1}{4}}^1V_{d-1}(1-t^4)^{\frac{d-1}{4}}dt\
<\int_0^1V_{d-1}(1-\frac{c^4}{d}-t^4)^{\frac{d-1}{4}}dt(*)
$$
From (a), we know
$$
V(H)\le(1-\frac{c^4}{d})^\frac{d}{4}\
\le e^{-\frac{c^4}{4}}\
&< e^{-\frac{c^4}{8}}.
$$
有点小寄，这题目老是想不到点上去…
