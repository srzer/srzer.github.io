About Me

About Me

💻 I am a fourth-year undergraduate at IIIS, Tsinghua University (a.k.a. Yao Class, directed by the Turing Award Laureate Andrew Chi-Chih Yao). I major in Computer Science and Technology, and minor in Chinese Language and Literature.

📚 My research interests lie broadly in machine learning. I have worked on topics related to foundation models toward alignment and decision making, especially their theoretical foundations in representation and optimization. Additionally, I am curious about how can AI guide biomedical discoveries.

⭐ I am fortunate to work with Prof. Simon S. Du at UW and Prof. Huazhe Xu at Tsinghua. During my visit to UW, I’ve also learned a lot from collaborations with Prof. Noah A. Smith and Prof. Hannaneh Hajishirzi.

👽 My mind is open. If you have any ideas which we both might be interested in, feel free to reach out. I am applying for a PhD position starting in 2025. Please contact me through email if you are interested in my research!
